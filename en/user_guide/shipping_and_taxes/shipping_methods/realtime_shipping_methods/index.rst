*************************
Realtime Shipping Methods
*************************

Here is the list of realtime shipping methods in CS-Cart & Multi-Vendor. By *real-time* we mean the shipping methods that automatically calculate shipping rates on their servers, depending on the information provided by the customer. These rates are then sent to the store, so that the customer could see them and make the decision what shipping method to use.
