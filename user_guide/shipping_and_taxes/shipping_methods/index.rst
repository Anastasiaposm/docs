****************
Shipping Methods
****************

A shipping method is a set of rules and parameters to calculate the cost of shipping orders to buyers. This section contains an editable list of shipping methods present in the store.

.. image:: img/shipping_methods.png
    :align: center
    :alt: Shipping methods list

Use this list to edit certain attributes of a shipping method. To access more attributes, click on the method name or the gear button of the required shipping method and choose **Edit** from the drop-down list.
