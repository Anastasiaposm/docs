*****
Taxes
*****

This section contains an editable list of tax calculation rules available in your store. Use this section to add new rules and manage existing.

.. image:: img/taxes.png
    :align: center
    :alt: Taxes

Actions on taxes
****************

*	To add new tax calculation rules, click the **+** button.
*	To edit existing tax calculation rules, click the gear button of the tax and choose **Edit**.
*	To apply multiple tax calculation rules to all products in the catalog, tick the checkboxes of the desired rules, click the gear button at the top of the page, and choose **Apply selected taxes to all products**.
*	To remove selected taxes from all products, tick the checkboxes of the desired rules, click the gear button at the top of the page, and choose **Remove selected taxes from all products**.
*	To delete a tax calculation rule, click the gear button of the rule and choose **Delete**.
*	To make tax calculation rule active or disabled. choose *Active* or *Disabled* in the **Status** field.
*	To specify rates, click the gear button of the required rule and choose **Edit**. Then switch to the **Tax rates** tab.
